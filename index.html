Contoh: Transaksi JPKSU BBJ (Daytrade)

Nasabah beli 3 lot di 36200. Harga turun, ia likuidasi 1 lot di 36100. Harga naik lagi, sisa 2 lot dilikuidasi di 36500.

Sesi 1 (Rugi):

P/L = [(36100 - 36200) x $5 x 1] - ($30 + $3,3) = -$500 - $33,3 = -$533,3

Sesi 2 (Untung):

P/L = [(36500 - 36200) x $5 x 2] - [($30 + $3,3) x 2] = $3000 - $66,6 = $2933,4

Total Keuntungan Bersih

$2400,7

Contoh: Transaksi XUL 10 (Overnight)

Nasabah beli 1 lot di $3175,30, lalu beli lagi 2 lot di $3160,40. Ia likuidasi 1 lot di $3170,50 (hari sama). Sisa 2 lot dilikuidasi besoknya di $3172,45.

Hari 1 (Untung):

P/L = [($3170,50 - $3160,40) x 100] - $33,3 = $976,7

Hari 2 (Mix) & Biaya:

Posisi 1 Rugi: -$318,3
Posisi 2 Untung: +$1171,7
Biaya Rollover: -$10

Total Keuntungan Bersih

$1830,1

A. Transaksi EU1010_BBJ

P/L = [(1,1240 - 1,1230) x 100.000 x 2] - 66,6 = $133,4

B. Transaksi UJ1010_BBJ
